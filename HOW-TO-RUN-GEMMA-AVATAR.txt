You can switch to `gemma4:26b` with just an environment variable at launch.

Use these 2 commands:
(1st command):
cd /home/l3ung/gemma-avatar

(2nd command):
OLLAMA_MODEL=gemma4:26b PATH="$HOME/.bun/bin:$PATH" ./run-all-local.sh

Then open Microsoft Edge to:
http://localhost:3000


Why this works:
1. The launch scripts already support model override via `OLLAMA_MODEL`.
2. If unset, they auto-pick from available Gemma models in fallback order.
3. No code/file changes are needed.

Relevant scripts:
- run-all-local.sh
- start-ollama.sh
- start-speech-to-speech.sh

What to expect after switching:
1. Same URL: `http://localhost:3000`
2. Usually faster/lighter than `31b`
3. Quality may be slightly lower than `31b` depending on prompt complexity
